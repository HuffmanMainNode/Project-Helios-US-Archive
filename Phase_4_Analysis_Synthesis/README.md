# Phase 4: Analysis & Synthesis

## Objectives
This phase focuses on transforming the interlinked knowledge graph into high-level insights through semantic analysis and synthesis.

## Methodology
1. **Semantic Relationship Extraction**: Leveraging NLP models to identify context-heavy relationships between legal precedents and historical events.
2. **Connection Strength Quantification**: Applying graph-based centrality measures to determine which documents or events act as pivotal nodes in the US Archive.
3. **Data Synthesis**: Combining vector-based similarity scores with graph structures to detect latent shifts in legal policy during major national crises.
4. **Insight Generation**: Building automated summaries that synthesize cross-referenced data into actionable research briefs.
