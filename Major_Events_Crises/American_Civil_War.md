# American Civil War

**Date:** 1861-04-12 to 1865-04-09
**Source Citation:** National Archives / Historical Consensus (Mock)

## Description
A civil war fought in the United States between the Union and the Confederacy, primarily over the status of slavery.

## Linked Legal Shifts
Led to the Reconstruction Amendments (13th, 14th, and 15th Amendments).
