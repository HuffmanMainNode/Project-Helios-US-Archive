# Federalist No 10

**Description:** An essay written by James Madison as the tenth of The Federalist Papers, discussing the danger of "factions".
**Source Citation:** [Library of Congress](https://guides.loc.gov/federalist-papers/text-1-10#s-lg-box-wrapper-25493273)
