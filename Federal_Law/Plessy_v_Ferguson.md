# Plessy v. Ferguson

**Date:** 1896-05-18
**Jurisdiction:** Federal
**Source Citation:** Caselaw Access Project (Mock)

## Summary
Landmark decision of the U.S. Supreme Court that upheld the constitutionality of racial segregation laws for public facilities as long as the segregated facilities were equal in quality.

## Text Snippet
> The "separate but equal" provision of private services mandated by state government is constitutional under the Equal Protection Clause.
