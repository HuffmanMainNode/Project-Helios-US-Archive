# Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA)

## Legal Summary
Extends government restrictions on wire taps from telephone calls to include transmissions of electronic data by computer.

## Statutory Snippet
> The ECPA was passed in 1986 to expand the Federal Wiretap Act of 1968, which was primarily designed to address wiretapping of landline telephones.
