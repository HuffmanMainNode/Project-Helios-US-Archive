# Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)

## Legal Summary
The primary federal statute used to prosecute hacking and unauthorized access to computers. It prohibits accessing a computer without authorization or exceeding authorized access.

## Statutory Snippet
> Whoever having accessed a computer without authorization or exceeding authorized access, and by means of such conduct having obtained information from any protected computer... shall be punished as provided in subsection (c) of this section.
